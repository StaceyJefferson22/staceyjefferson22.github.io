What is Styling Trends of Elegance?

This business is home to the AVON Products you will find on the companies' website and advertisements. AVON is the company both Sowell and Jefferson work under and has been acknowledge a Fortune 500 company and one of the Interbrand's Best Global Brand. Their motto is "AVON, make dreams come true and so can you."

Sondra Sowell and Sharon Jefferson are no strangers to AVON as they have achieve awards and accolades from the company. They also have a vast knowledge of every AVON Product currently and will help you in achieving maximum beauty both on the inside and outside.
